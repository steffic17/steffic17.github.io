Data Analysis & Data Processing

  • DATA CLEANSING
  • STATISTICAL ANALYSIS
  • DATA VISUALISATION
  • DATA MANIPULATION

We can help you understand your data! Release the meaningful stories from your huge lists of numbers and groupings. How do we do this?

First, we understand what you are measuring, is it categorical or numeric data or a combination. We then cleanse the data for format, anomalies, validity, completeness and consistency.

At this point we can visualise your data; histograms, bar charts, box and whisker plots. We can also do some statistical assessments of the data, to find distributions, maximum values, minimum values, means, medians. All as appropriate for the data.

Now the data can be manipulated, have the data streams combined, variables compared, or machine learning tools applied to reveal deeper insights.
